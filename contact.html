Meet Our Team

Ricky Reeves - CEO

I am Ricky, the CEO and founder of Tech Ability.

In 2006, I was diagnosed with cancer that unfortunately resulted in legal blindness. However, my passion for technology has only intensified. With a focus on accessibility, I have honed my skills on Windows computers using screen readers and have since transitioned to Apple products, mastering their features and tricks.

My ultimate goal is to establish Tech Ability as the leading institution for individuals to learn a wide range of technology skills and confidently navigate the digital world.

Thomas Bernard - IT Admin

As the IT Admin at Tech Ability, I manage our social media, website, and backend tools, ensuring everything runs smoothly behind the scenes. I'm also here to assist customers and respond to emails, always ready to help with any tech-related questions.

Outside of work, I'm passionate about amateur radio and host a weekly radio show on Plains FM. I also enjoy honing my public speaking skills as a member of Toastmasters.

Jordan Langley – Weather Enthusiast & Tech Specialist

I'm passionate about both weather and technology, and I love sharing local weather updates through Facebook and on the radio, including my show on Plains FM. Outside of weather reporting, I enjoy exploring the South Island by bus and taxi.

As someone who is legally blind with low vision, I have a deep understanding of the importance of adaptive technologies and assistive tools. I specialize in helping others with assistive devices, smart technology, and iPhones, ensuring everyone can stay connected and empowered through tech.
